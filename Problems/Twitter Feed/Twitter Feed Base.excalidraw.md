### Functional Requirements
- Scroll home feed (timeline)
- Create a post (tweet)
- Like a post ^rf3sU3WO

### Non-Functional Requirements
- High availability (AP) for feed reads
- Read >> Write (10,000:1+)
- Low latency feed loading
- Fan-out at scale: celebrity with 10M followers posts → 10M feeds ^T47pnmly

### Out of Scope
- Retweets / reposts
- Comments / replies
- Direct messages
- Search / trending
- Ranking algorithm ^VyfVZrwA

- Follow
- FeedItem ^1g4voyKc
